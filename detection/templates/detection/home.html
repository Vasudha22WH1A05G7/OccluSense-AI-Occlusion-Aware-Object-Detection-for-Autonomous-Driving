NMS Methods for Occlusion Handling

Choose the right algorithm for your detection scenario

Standard NMS

Traditional Non-Maximum Suppression with hard thresholding. Fast and efficient for well-separated objects.

  • Hard suppression
  • Fastest processing
  • IoU-based filtering
Recommended

Soft-NMS

Gaussian decay instead of hard removal. Better handles overlapping objects by reducing scores instead of eliminating.

  • Gaussian score decay
  • Configurable sigma
  • Better for crowds

DIoU-NMS

Distance-IoU considers center point distance. Ideal for occluded objects where boxes overlap but centers are distant.

  • Center distance aware
  • Better occlusion handling
  • Robust suppression
